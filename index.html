About Me

I am a Computer Science PhD student at Stony Brook University. I am a member of the File Systems and Storage Lab (FSL) and I am advised by Prof. Erez Zadok. My research interests are in distributed systems, cloud computing, and storage systems. I am currently working on designing distributed, secure and reliable clusters for running machine learning applications. This envolves setting up runtime applications like OpenShift and Kubernetes on top of CephFS, NFS, and other distributed file systems.
