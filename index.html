Biography

I was born on August 25th 2001 in Mashhad city of Iran. Got my diploma for Mathematical and Physics at the age of 18 and then attended university for Computer Engineering.

I began my journey by learning and coding with C programming language. After that I started to learn Java programming language.

After one year at university, I started to work as a Front-end developer, using Vue.js. Then I worked on the WordPress systems and became a master in it. My first project was SSC (Student Scientific Society of Computer department) official website.

After that I started to learn python, flask and django, to put myself in the back-end developing path. After 3 months, I joined the Maktab Sharif PHP/Laravel bootcamp to learn PHP. I worked as a trainee for 6 months, and then I learn core PHP developing and Laravel framework. After that I joined Snapp! company and started my position as Golang back-end trainee. I have been working as a back-end developer for almost 7 months.
